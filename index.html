ABOUT

I have been attending Northeastern University since fall of 2012, and am a Computer Science and Interactive Media combined major.

I enjoy anime, animation, video games, computers, good food, and good music. I like to appreciate what's given to me in life, do my best when I'm in a sticky situation, and work diligently to achieve my goals.

Sometimes, I like to pretend I'm as wise as a sage, giving what I deem as good advice to others. For me, giving advice is a pretty fun time because I have fun helping people if I can be of assistance to them. Volunteering is also something that is of interest to me, because it's another form of helping others that lets me know that I am helping to repair the world.

Some places in the world that I'd like to visit are Japan, and Finland, if I ever get the chance to. I have been to Israel, which was also one of my desired destinations! I went this summer on a program called Onward Israel. My blog is featured in the 'Websites' section.

If you've read this far, one last thing I'd like to say, is: please enjoy my portfolio!

Games + Apps

MP3 Player

This MP3 player is completely customizable with XML! Some features of this player are: it displays extended song information for a song that is clicked on, a user can sort songs by four different categories, pause/rewind/next song controls are implemented, current time progress for each song is tracked, and there is a page system. Since it takes such a long time to download from Github, I'm working on featuring it somewhere on this website.

See the MP3 Player on Github!
